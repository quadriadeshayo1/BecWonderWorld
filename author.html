Author

BLESSING E. CHUKWU hails from ihie in isiala Ngwa North Local Government Area of Abia state. He attended Hope High School, Lagos and Methodist Boy's High School, Lagos for his H.S.C. programme. He is a product of the university of ibadan


BLESSING E. CHUKWU writes children and youth programmes for radio,television,newspaper and magazines. He runs educational programmes in private, local and international schools in Lagos State.He has introduced a CREATIVE WRITING PROGRAMME at the primary school level by organizing the the young writers'club in reputable international schools in lagos


In 1997, Blessing created and co-ordinated the first ever nationwide short story competition from primary to universities level.He is the founder and first co-ordinator of Association of School Clubs and Society in Lagos School Management Committe now (L.E.D). He is the Executive Co-ordinator of the young writers'club and BEC WONDER WORLD, LAGOS


He has written many children and youth work under publication. Some of his published works are Oluchi and the monster, A reward of life, Nursery Rhymes and other poems, and Guided Creative Writing Series Books (Book 1-6), Vocabulary Development Series(Book 1-6). He is a vesatile and prolific writer. Blessing is happily married with children.

Vocabulary development

Vocabulary Development Series

Vocabulary is fundamental to every aspect of language- Listening,speaking, reading and writing.This basic language skills are built upon pupils' knowledge and control over words. It is clear that the teaching of vocabulary is crucial to helping pupil become more confident users of English.The activities in this series was created to help pupils feel confident about their vocabulary skills and to help them understand the steps involve in analyzing and mastering new words. Practice they say makes perfect. You can only know what you should know only if you make effort to know.

Creative writing

Creative writing series

Basic steps in creative writing will challenge your pupil's/children's imagination and writing skills by providing pictures and writing activities to enhance logical and creative thinking and writing skills.This workbook series provides your children the opportunites to write in a variety of topic and themes. It will broaden and deepen thier imagination and guide them to express the same in comprehensive writing. The result of human imagination are unimaginable and unlimited.The tangible things of life were born out of human imaginations. It is therefore imperative for children to begin the process of developing their imaginations at tender ages. This workbook series will systematically inculcate in them the basic steps and the unique features of a short story or novel which will eventually make them better creative writers

Writing with soryboards

Writing with storyboards

For grade six - nine

Writing with storyboards was conceived and packaged to enable students/pupils in primary and secondary schools to assess themselves of how much they think they know about creative writing (story writing) and challenge them to improve thier literary writing skills. It is meant for schools as creative writing assessment books and for private use at home.
I believe that every knowledge and human activity should end with the production or achievement of something tangible or intangible. Therefore the knowledge of creative writing as a course of study and a process to translate into the production of literary works. This workbook provides briefs explanation of the main elements, concepts, styles and technique of a story.
